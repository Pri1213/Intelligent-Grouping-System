What is the website about

The Intelligent Students Grouping Software is a website designed by a a group of four members in the hopes of trying to make life easier for lecturers when creating groups ammong in group assignments. Basically this website will enable admins to come up with their personality types in relation to their students as well as a survey which will be answered by the students in order to establish the personality traits. Upon completion of the survey, the traits will be uploaded, secondly their academic records will also be used in forming these groups. The website will use an Artificial Intelligence algorithm that will group different types of students, a good example is, the system will try match a student who has leadership qualities, and extroverted to a student whose introverted so as to create a balance and hopefully in the process the students learn one or two qualities from each other.
