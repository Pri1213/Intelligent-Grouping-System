Welcome to Intelligent Students Grouping Software!

This page will help you to know more about this website.

Our software is designed to help Charles Telfair Institute create groups of students based on their dimension analysis.


Our team is made up of good developers. We believe that every student deserves the chance to express themselves through a survey, and our software is designed to help make that a reality.

Our software is designed to be easy to use, with an intuitive interface that can be customized to meet the unique needs for CTI. Whether you are looking to group students by different dimensions, our software can help you create the optimal learning environment for each student.


Thank you for choosing Intelligent Student Grouping Software! We're excited to be a part of your educational journey.
